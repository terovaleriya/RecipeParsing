Moroccan minced lamb

Ingredients

1 Waitrose Organic Onion, chopped
400g pack Duchy Originals From Waitrose Organic Welsh Lamb Mince
Â½ tsp Cooks' Ingredients Organic Ground Cinnamon
400g can Waitrose Organic Chopped Tomatoes
100g Waitrose Organic Dried
Pitted Apricots, chopped
50g essential Waitrose Pitted Green Olives, halved
2 preserved lemons, chopped
250g Waitrose Organic Couscous

Method

1.Fry the onion and lamb in a large saucepan for 5 minutes to brown. Stir in the cinnamon, tomatoes, apricots, olives, lemon and 100ml water and simmer for 10 minutes, covered, stirring occasionally.

2. Meanwhile, place the couscous in a bowl and pour over 300ml boiling water, cover and stand for 5 minutes. Fluff up with a fork and serve with the Moroccan minced lamb.
