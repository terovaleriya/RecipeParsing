Cinnamon Pancakes with Honey Glazed Bananas

Honey and bananas taste wonderful together. Serve these sweet pancakes with virtually fat free fromage frais and a dusting of icing sugar for an irresistible low-fat dessert. You will find the dried bananas in the fruit and vegetable section.

Ingredients

For the pancakes:
100g plain flour
Pinch of salt
Â½ tsp ground cinnamon
1 medium egg, beaten
300ml skimmed milk
A little sunflower oil, for greasing

For the topping:
3 tbsp Waitrose Finest West Indian Rum (or apple juice)
2 x 125g packs Waitrose Dried Small Bananas
4 tbsp clear honey

To serve:
Icing sugar, for dusting
4 tbsp Waitrose Virtually Fat Free Fromage Frais

Method

  1. For the pancake topping, place the rum in a small saucepan and heat gently, then remove from the heat and stir in the bananas. Leave to stand for 20 minutes, or until the bananas have absorbed the rum.
  2. Meanwhile, to make the pancake batter, sift the flour, salt and cinnamon into a bowl and make a well in the centre. Whisk in the egg and gradually beat in the milk, drawing in the flour from the sides to make a smooth batter.
  3. Use kitchen paper dipped in oil to lightly oil a 20cm heavy-based non-stick frying pan. Add just enough batter to thinly coat the pan's base and cook over a moderate heat for about 1 minute, or until golden brown. Turn over and cook the other side until golden brown.
  4. Transfer to a warm plate and cover with a square of greaseproof paper. Repeat with the remaining batter to make 8 pancakes. Keep warm by placing them in the oven at 100°C, gas mark 1⁄2, until required.
  5. Pour the honey over the rum-soaked bananas and cook gently, stirring occasionally, for 4-5 minutes, until the bananas have softened slightly and are caramelised. Serve 2 pancakes per person, folded in half, then in half again, and place the bananas and syrup on top. Dust with icing sugar and serve with 1 tablespoon of the fromage frais.

Cook's tips

Using a pastry brush or kitchen paper to lightly oil the pan, rather than pouring the oil straight in, helps to reduce the fat content of the pancakes.

The honey-glazed bananas also make a lovely topping for low-fat yogurt.

Variation

This recipe also works well with 2 x 100g packs Waitrose Dried Apple Rings or a 200g pack Waitrose Semi-Dried Large Figs, cut in half. Both are available in the fruit and vegetable section.
