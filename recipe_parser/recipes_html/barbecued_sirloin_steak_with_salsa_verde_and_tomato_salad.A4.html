Barbecued Sirloin Steak with Salsa Verde and Tomato Salad

Sirloin is a very tasty, juicy steak, which is great for barbecuing. Here it is served with salsa verde and a colourful salad of red and yellow tomatoes - a perfect dish for al fresco dining or as part of a barbecue.

Ingredients

  • 2 Waitrose Aberdeen Angus Sirloin Steaks, each weighing approximately 200g, from the Service Counter
  • The salsa verde:
  • 2 tbsp Opie capers, drained
  • 2 garlic cloves, peeled and roughly chopped
  • 2 Waitrose Anchovy Fillets, chopped
  • 1 pack Waitrose Fresh Basil
  • 2 tbsp parsley, roughly chopped
  • 4 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 tbsp fresh lemon juice
  • Freshly ground black pepper

Method

  1. Preheat the barbecue or grill.
  2. In a blender, whizz together all the salsa ingredients to make a smooth sauce
  3. Score one side of each steak in a criss-cross pattern with a sharp knife, cutting only about one third of the way into the surface. Spread 1 tbsp of the salsa over each scored side.
  4. Cook the steaks for 3-5 minutes on each side until cooked to your liking.
  5. Serve the steaks with an extra spoonful of salsa verde, a salad of red and yellow cherry tomatoes and buttered steamed new potatoes.

Cook's tips

As a rough guide to cooking steaks, allow 3 minutes cooking each side for rare; 4 minutes each side for medium; 5 minutes each side for well done.
