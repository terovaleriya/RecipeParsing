Festive whisky cocktails

Whisky with warming ginger and orange, great for Christmas parties.

The combination of Johnnie Walker Red Label Whisky with fiery ginger beer makes a perfect long cocktail for Christmas drinks parties. Or try it paired with an aromatic orange liqueur for a delicious after-dinner digestif.

Ingredients

Ginger storm cocktail

2 tbsp Johnnie Walker Red Label Whisky
Dash Angostura Bitters
200ml Waitrose Fiery Ginger Beer

Whisky twist

3 ice cubes
2 tbsp Johnnie Walker Red Label Whisky
2 tbsp orange liqueur, such as Grand Marnier or Cointreau
Twist of orange peel, to serve

Method

Ginger storm

1. Pour the whisky into a champagne flute and add a dash of Angostura Bitters.

2. Top up with ginger beer and serve swiftly.

Whisky twist

1. Place the ice in a small tumbler and pour over the whisky and orange liqueur.

2. Muddle together, add a twist of orange peel and serve.

Cocktail tip

Try adding 2 tbsp sweet vermouth in place of the Angostura Bitters.
