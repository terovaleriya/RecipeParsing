Grilled breakfast papaya

  • Vegetarian

Ingredients

1 large ripe papaya
1 unwaxed lime
4 tbsp light brown
soft sugar
160g coconut or plain yogurt
40-80g granola

Method

1 Preheat the grill to medium. Halve the papaya, discard the seeds and put the fruit in a snug-fitting dish. Zest and juice the lime into a small bowl. Stir in the sugar, then spoon the mixture over the papaya. Grill for 8-10 minutes, until the top is caramelised and brown. Serve with a generous dollop of yogurt, topped with granola.
