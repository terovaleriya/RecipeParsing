Tricolore pasta salad

1 of your 5 a day per serving 

  • Vegetarian

Ingredients

500g pack essential Waitrose Cherry Tomatoes
2 cloves garlic, chopped
2 tbsp essential Waitrose Olive Oil
4 essential Waitrose Garlic Slices
300g essential Waitrose Amori pasta
125g pack essential Waitrose Italian Mozzarella, torn
1 essential Waitrose Avocado, roughly diced 

Method

1. Preheat the oven to 200oC, gas mark 6.

2. Place the tomatoes, garlic and oil in a small roasting tin and roast for 15â€“20 minutes, adding the garlic bread to the oven for the last 10 minutes.

3. Meanwhile, cook the pasta according to pack instructions, drain and toss in the tomatoes. Add the mozzarella and avocado. Tear up the garlic bread and gently toss into the pasta and serve immediately.
