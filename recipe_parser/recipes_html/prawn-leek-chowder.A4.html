Prawn & leek chowder

  • Low Fat

Ingredients

2 tsp vegetable oil
150g essential Waitrose new potatoes, cut into 2cm dice
1 leek, sliced
400ml Cooks’ Ingredients Vegetable Stock
195g can essential Waitrose Sweetcorn in water, drained
½ x 235g pack frozen essential Waitrose Raw King Prawns
200ml semi-skimmed milk

Method

1. Heat the oil in a medium saucepan, add the diced potato and the sliced leek and fry for 5 minutes. Add the stock and the sweetcorn, then bring to the boil, cover and simmer for 5 minutes.

2. Add the king prawns and milk to the vegetable mixture and cook for a further 5 minutes until the potatoes are tender and the prawns are cooked right through.
