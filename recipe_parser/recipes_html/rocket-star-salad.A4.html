Rocket star salad

Ingredients

90g bag wild rocket
250g pouch Tilda Quinoa, Pumpkin & Sunflower Wholegrain Brown Basmati Rice, prepared according to pack instructions
1 ripe avocado halved, stoned and diced
Â½ lemon, juice
2 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
2 tbsp pumpkin seeds or roughly chopped nuts
125g raspberries
125g blueberries

Method

1. Put the rocket, grain mix and avocado in a large serving bowl. Add the lemon juice and oil and gently toss together. 

2. Scatter over the seeds or nuts and top with the berries

This recipe first appeared in Waitrose & Partners Health, New Year 2019 issue.
