Grapefruit, avocado & smoked salmon salad recipe | Waitrose

A bright and refreshing winter salad, perfectly virtuous as a light lunch to make up for that odd moment of over-indulgence
 

  • Gluten Free

Ingredients

1 pink grapefruit
28g pack coriander, stalks finely chopped, leaves picked
2 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
2 tsp light brown soft sugar
1 Waitrose 1 Perfectly Ripe Avocado, sliced
200g smoked salmon, roughly torn
2 tbsp Waitrose Love Life Mixed Seeds
 

Method

1. Finely grate the grapefruit zest into a large bowl then remove the peel and segment the fruit over the bowl to catch the juice (squeeze all the excess pith to extract as much juice as possible). Stir through the coriander stalks, olive oil and sugar then season.

2. When ready to serve, toss the avocado, salmon and most of the coriander leaves through the dressing. Serve immediately on a platter or individual plates, scattering over the mixed seeds and reserved coriander leaves.
