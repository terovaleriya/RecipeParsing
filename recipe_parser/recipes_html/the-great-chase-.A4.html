The great Chase cocktail recipe | Waitrose

This signature cocktail from an award-winning English vodka producer makes a brilliant welcome cocktail. Guaranteed to instantly put everyone in a celebratory mood.

  • Christmas

Ingredients

37ml Chase Vodka
12ml Chase Elderflower Liqueur
75ml soda water
Lemon wedge, to garnish 

Method

Pour the vodka, elderflower liqueur and soda water into a large ice-filled wine glass. Garnish with a lemon wedge.
